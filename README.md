# Harnessing: A History of Human Productivity

## 驾驭：一部人类生产力史

> Every leap in human civilization is a story of harnessing a new force — from oxen to engines, from electricity to algorithms, and now, from language models to autonomous agents.

---

## About This Book

This is an open-source book that traces humanity's journey of harnessing ever-greater sources of power and intelligence to amplify productivity. The narrative arc spans from the first stone tools to the rise of AI Agents, arguing that **Large Language Models and AI Agents represent the latest — and perhaps most transformative — chapter in this ongoing story of harnessing**.

The word "harness" carries a deliberate double meaning:
1. **To harness** (verb): to bring under control and direct the force of — as humans have done with animals, wind, steam, electricity, and computation.
2. **A harness** (noun): the framework/architecture that connects an LLM to tools, memory, and the real world — the defining structure of an AI Agent.

## 关于本书

这是一本开源书籍，追溯人类驾驭新力量以放大生产力的全部历程。叙事从最早的石器延伸到 AI Agent 的崛起，核心论点是：**大语言模型和 AI 智能体是人类"驾驭"史上最新、也可能是最具变革性的一章。**

"驾驭"一词承载双重含义：
1. **动词**：使之受控并引导其力量——正如人类对畜力、风力、蒸汽、电力和算力所做的。
2. **名词 (Harness)**：将 LLM 连接到工具、记忆和真实世界的框架/架构——AI Agent 的核心结构。
